Principal Consultant

Dr Patrick Hickey is Principal Consultant at Opuspath. He has 25 years of experience as a management consultant. Prior to his consulting career he was Executive Director at CTVR. CTVR was a research consortium headquartered at Trinity College Dublin with over 100 PhD post Docs and students located in various universities around Ireland. Prior to that he held a number of senior management positions with Intel Ireland. He holds a DBA, MBA,BA and a Dipl;oma in Industrial Engineering. Patrick is also a certified six sigma black belt and is a Prince2 PM practitioner.
